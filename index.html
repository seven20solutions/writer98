Keyboard Shortcuts

Here’s how to work quickly in the editor, Word 98 style:

  • Ctrl/Cmd+B – Bold
  • Ctrl/Cmd+I – Italic
  • Ctrl/Cmd+U – Underline
  • Ctrl/Cmd+N – New document
  • Ctrl/Cmd+S – Save (auto-save)
  • Ctrl/Cmd+P – Print
  • Ctrl/Cmd+L/E/R – Align left/center/right
  • Ctrl/Cmd+Shift+8 – Bulleted list
  • Ctrl/Cmd+Shift+7 – Numbered list
  • Ctrl/Cmd+1/2/3 – Heading 1/2/3

Press Esc or the Close button to return to the editor.
